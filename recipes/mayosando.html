Mayo Sando

The Easiest, Fastest Late Night Snack

The Mayo Sandwich is guaranteed to settle your empty belly with the perfect balance of ingredients, the best thing about the Mayo Sando is that, it is perfect on it's own, while still being entirely customizable and turned gourmet with simple, found in fridge ingredients. But first, let's start with the basics!

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread (it is best with plain white bread, but can be substituted)
  • 1 cup of mayonaise
  • 1 tsp of salt
  • 1 tsp of black pepper

How to Build your Mayo Sando

  1. Lather 1/4 of a cup of mayonaise on each side of the bread (yes, this includes the outside, trust me! Or don't!)
  2. Sprinkle salt and peppper onto the inside of your sandwich
  3. Get a pan, preferably a cast iron, and heat it up over your stove
  4. Once your pan is nice and hot, add your sandwich, flipping once when golden brown (or burnt for added exquisite flavors)
  5. Remove from pan and simply enjoy! Let your tastebuds and imagination sink into the crispy and velvety texture

If you aren't in a rush and looking to spice things up, feel free to add whatever you have in your fridge to elevate your mayo sando! Adding ingredients such as pineapples, mustard, ice cream, soy sauce, and more can easily bring your mayo sando to the next level! Let your imagination run wild and the possibilities are endless!
