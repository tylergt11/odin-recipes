Nachos

The Best Snacks Should Take Little to No Effort

Watching a sports game at home? Want to feel like you're tailgating at the stadium with a cold one and some proper stadium food? Look no further! This is the easiest recipe to make you feel like you're surrounded by screaming fans! Our nachos have got you covered!

Ingredients

  • Tortilla Chips (or any chips, if you don't have chips, plain cereal also works)
  • Kraft Singles (you know them, you love them!)

How to Build your Nachos

  1. Add your choice of chips to a plate
  2. Throw on 1, 2, 3, 8 Kraft Singles on top of the chips
  3. Pop that baby into the microwave for 15 seconds
  4. Crack a cold one, sit back, relax, and enjoy!

Decorate them with however you'd like, top it off with some hot sauce, throw on some jalapenos, add beans, whatever your heart desires!
